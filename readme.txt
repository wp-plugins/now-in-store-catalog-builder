Easily create beautiful marketing materials from your ecommerce shop with Now In Store. Create catalogs and line sheets in minutes.

== Description ==

Our catalog and flyer builder automatically creates professional and customizable catalogs and flyers for
your WooCommerce listings in under 30 seconds.

Our blog and online-chat experts are on hand that share tips and tricks on how you can use catalogs and
flyers to grow your sales.

With a few clicks, import all of your WooCommerce products into a beautiful drag and drop interface. Easily
choose and organize each page of your catalog or flyer, use different layouts, customize your front and
back covers and add a wholesale contract and order forms.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the archive inside the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
